Does Kiri AI work on any website?
Yes. Kiri AI works on any website — WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, Squarespace, Wix, custom HTML, or any framework. You just paste one line of JavaScript into your site's <head> or body and it's live.
Can I customize how the widget looks?
Absolutely. You can change the chat bubble color, header branding, bot avatar, welcome message, button position, and font — all from the dashboard without touching code. Pro and Enterprise plans also support full white-labeling.
Can a human agent take over a conversation?
Yes — that's one of Kiri's best features. On the Pro plan, any agent can click "Take Over" in the inbox and step into the conversation. The customer sees a smooth transition and the agent gets the full chat history. The AI steps aside automatically.
Do I need to know how to code?
No coding skills required. The dashboard walks you through everything — training your bot, customizing the widget, and installing it. Installation is a single copy-paste. If you can edit your website, you can install Kiri AI.
What happens after I buy the Pro plan?
You're upgraded instantly. All Pro features unlock immediately — unlimited conversations, human takeover, advanced analytics, and more. You'll receive a receipt via email and can manage your billing anytime from your account settings. Cancel anytime, no penalty.
How does Kiri AI learn about my business?
You can train Kiri by pasting your website URL (it crawls the content automatically), uploading PDF documents, or manually entering Q&A pairs. The more you train it, the more accurate it becomes. Training updates are live within seconds.
